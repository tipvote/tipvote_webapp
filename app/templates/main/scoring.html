Transparent Scoring

We want to give transparent fair scoring while combating "Hive Mind Syndrome". We also believe crypto allows the poor to be heard. Please not this will be changed as things become not what we envisioned.

What is "Hive Mind Scoring"?
Downvotes are a problem

  • If a person gets downvoted it gets followed by more downvotes.
  • Opinions that are not in the majority get downvoted and hidden.
  • People afraid to speak out against the majority opinion in fear of getting downvoted.
Current Scoring
Upvotes: 2 Point
Downvotes: 1 Point
Comments: 2 Points
Notice a downvote gives a point :)
Crypto Influence
Promotions have a great effect on the points, while tipping has some. Here is the formula.
Tipping Scoring:

0-1 USD = 5 points
1USD = 5 points
Therefore 500 USD = 2500 points
Promotion Scoring:

0-1 USD = 5 points
1USD = 50 points
Therefore 500 USD = 25000 points
Tipvote Coins
Tipvote Coins have there own built in points. A post be given the coins by the poster or other people. They are earned by using the site for people who cant afford to use crypto. We intend to keep it free to help even the poorest voices be heard
